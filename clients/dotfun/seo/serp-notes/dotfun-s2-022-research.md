## Coverage gaps to exploit

- Competitors mostly explain the feature. We should explain the operating decision: when a text is enough, when AI reception is better, and when a human should take over.
- Competitors often jump to templates. We should start with call intent and speed-to-lead risk.
- Competitors under-cover CRM handoff and owner notifications. Iris differentiation lives there.
- Competitors sometimes overstate recovery/conversion rates. We should avoid fabricated percentages and focus on practical measurement.
- Compliance is often treated as a footnote. We should treat it as a workflow design constraint.

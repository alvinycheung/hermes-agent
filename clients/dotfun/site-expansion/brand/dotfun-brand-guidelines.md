## Confirmed brand architecture

- dotfun is the parent brand.
- Iris is software developed by dotfun.
- Public wings:
  - Iris Platform
  - dotfun AI Solutions
  - dotfun Growth Services

## Voice

- dotfun: playful-professional — human, direct, strategic, transparent, anti-fluff, collaborative, practical.
- Iris: operator-sharp — clear, confident, modern, precise, executive-ready, still human and witty.

## Core tagline territory

AI growth automation, built by growth operators.

## Tone rules

- Clear over clever.
- Respectfully rebellious toward legacy tools.
- Human-first AI; do not imply fully autonomous replacement of teams.
- Services should feel scarce and senior.
- Avoid generic AI/corporate language.

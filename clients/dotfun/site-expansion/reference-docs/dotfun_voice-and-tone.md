## **Voice and tone evolution**

Iris should keep dotfun’s core personality:

* Direct  
* Human  
* Strategic  
* Transparent  
* Fun without being unserious  
* Anti-fluff  
* Collaborative  
* Practical

But Iris needs a more mature surface layer for funded startups, mid-market B2B buyers, RevOps leaders, investors, and AI-forward operators.

### **dotfun voice**

Playful-professional.

“Write like a human. Talk like a partner. Skip the BS. Make it fun.”

### **Iris voice**

Operator-sharp.

Clear, confident, modern, and direct. Still human. Still witty. Still allergic to corporate nonsense. But a little more precise, a little more executive-ready, and a little less agency-casual.

### **Voice attributes**

#### **1\. Operator-built**

We speak from experience, not theory.

We know the pain because we have run the campaigns, built the dashboards, fixed the workflows, cleaned the data, answered to leadership, and carried the number.

**Sounds like:**

* We’ve lived in the mess.  
* Built by people who actually run growth.  
* Strategy is cute. Execution is where things get real.

**Does not sound like:**

* Our innovative solutions leverage cutting-edge AI.  
* We transform your go-to-market paradigm.

---

#### **2\. Clear over clever**

We can be witty, but clarity wins.

Iris should explain complex AI and growth systems in language a smart operator can understand quickly.

**Sounds like:**

* Capture demand. Automate follow-up. See what is working.  
* AI belongs in the workflow, not in a separate tab nobody uses.

**Does not sound like:**

* Agentic orchestration across cross-functional value streams.  
* AI-powered synergy for scalable customer-centric outcomes.

---

#### **3\. Respectfully rebellious**

We challenge legacy systems without sounding bitter.

The tone is not “everything old is stupid.”

The tone is “the old model got us here, but it will not get us where we are going.”

**Sounds like:**

* Your CRM had a good run. We’ll take it from here.  
* Legacy tools helped define the last era. Iris is built for the next one.

**Does not sound like:**

* HubSpot is dead.  
* Salesforce is trash.

---

#### **4\. Human-first AI**

AI should make teams more capable, not less thoughtful.

We believe in automation, but not soulless automation. The system should handle repetitive work, surface insight, and support better decisions.

**Sounds like:**

* Let AI handle the repetitive work so humans can do the work that actually needs judgment.  
* Automate the obvious. Elevate the important. 

**Does not sound like:**

* Replace your entire team with agents.  
* Fully autonomous growth with no humans required.

---

#### **5\. Scarce and senior**

For services, Iris should feel selective.

The company is not trying to become a volume agency. It is building software while selectively partnering with premium clients.

**Sounds like:**

* We only take on a few Iris Growth clients at a time.  
* Senior operators, embedded where they can actually move the number.

**Does not sound like:**

* We offer affordable marketing services for everyone.  
* Book a free consultation with our team of specialists.

---

## **Tone by context**

### **Homepage**

Confident, category-defining, plainspoken.

**Goal:** Make Iris feel like the next-generation answer to bloated GTM software.

**Tone:** Bold but not breathless.

**Example:**

AI growth automation, built by growth operators.

Iris helps modern teams capture demand, automate follow-up, and build revenue systems without the bloat of legacy CRM.

---

### **Product pages**

Practical, benefit-led, specific.

**Goal:** Explain what the product does and why it matters.

**Tone:** Clear, useful, direct.

**Example:**

Your website, phone, and SMS channels should not create three different follow-up problems. Iris brings conversations into one AI-powered system so leads get answered, qualified, and routed faster.

---

### **AI services pages**

Consultative, confident, grounded.

**Goal:** Make custom AI feel safe, practical, and tied to business outcomes.

**Tone:** Expert but not over-engineered.

**Example:**

Most teams do not need an AI strategy deck. They need one workflow that saves 10 hours a week, then another, then another. Iris Agents helps you find, build, and launch the automations that actually matter.

---

### **Growth services pages**

Selective, senior, outcome-focused.

**Goal:** Position Iris Growth as premium and scarce.

**Tone:** Confident, experienced, high-touch.

**Example:**

Iris Growth is not a volume agency. We work with a small number of companies where senior strategy, strong execution, and AI-enabled systems can create meaningful revenue impact.

---

### **Investor narrative**

Strategic, model-driven, crisp.

**Goal:** Show how software and services reinforce each other.

**Tone:** Clear and compelling.

**Example:**

Iris uses high-value services as a paid discovery engine. We solve real growth and operations problems for clients, identify repeatable patterns, and turn the best workflows into platform features.

---

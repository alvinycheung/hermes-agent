The company is evolving from dotfun’s founder-led growth, design, and operations model into a services-enabled AI growth automation company. The near-term product wedge is AI software for SMBs, including AI CRM, web chat, SMS, and AI receptionist/front desk capabilities. The longer-term company direction is: AI growth automation for funded startups, mid-market B2B teams, GTM teams, marketing teams, and operators who need smarter systems without the bloat of legacy GTM software.

**AI-native software | Custom AI agents and automation | Operator-led growth marketing services**

That combination is the wedge. Iris is software built by people who have actually run growth.

## **COMPETITIVE LANDSCAPE**

### **Core stance**

Respect the legacy. Reject the legacy model.

HubSpot, Marketo, Salesforce, and the broader CRM/marketing automation category helped define the last era of go-to-market software.

We are not here to pretend those tools did not matter. But the next era needs something different.

It needs less bloat, fewer bolt-ons, more useful automation, clearer data, and AI that is built into the work instead of trapped behind confusing pricing, credits, or enterprise packaging.

### **Competitive philosophy**

Iris should not position as angry. Iris should position as inevitable.

The tone is:

*Thanks for building the last era of go to market tools. We’ll take it from here.*

### **Against legacy CRMs**

Legacy CRMs became systems of record. Iris is building a system of action.

Legacy CRMs store customer data. Iris helps teams act on it.

Legacy CRMs were built for every possible business. Iris is built for modern growth teams that need capture, follow-up, automation, insight, and execution.

### **Against marketing automation suites**

Traditional marketing automation platforms are powerful, but they often require heavy setup, expensive tiers, and specialized admins.

Iris is built for teams that want growth automation without needing a separate operations department just to make the software useful.

### **Against AI CRM startups**

Many AI CRM startups focus on the database.

Iris focuses on the growth system.

The CRM matters, but it is only one layer. The real opportunity is connecting CRM, communication, automation, content, intelligence, reporting, and execution into one growth operating system.

### **Against AI agencies and consultants**

AI consultants can build interesting workflows, but many stop at the custom build.

Iris turns repeatable services work into scalable software.

That means clients get white-glove strategy and implementation, while the platform keeps getting smarter from real-world growth problems.
